How to read it. Narrow the wings toward $100 and the needle slides to the center — net theta hits zero because the decay you collect on the sold wings matches the decay you pay on the body. What's left is the payoff valley: you lose the debit if the stock pins at $100, and you bank capped profit if it breaks past either wing. Killing theta doesn't make a free trade — it trades the daily clock for needing a real move.
